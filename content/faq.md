## What is Reminders+?

Reminders+ is a modern task and routine app designed to help you build consistency. Instead of overwhelming to-do lists, it focuses on one simple principle: complete at least one task every day and keep your streak alive.

## How does the streak system work?

Every day you complete at least one task, your streak increases by one. Miss a day, and it resets to zero. This simple mechanism motivates you to stay productive and build lasting habits through consistent daily progress.
